=== Company Overview ===
Acme Technologies is a Bengaluru-based startup specializing in AI-driven customer support.
The company was founded in 2019 and has 120 employees across India, Singapore, and the US.

=== Products ===
1. AcmeChat — An AI-powered chat assistant for websites and mobile apps.
2. AcmeDocs — A knowledge management system that uses embeddings for document search.
3. AcmeVoice — A voice AI for call centers.

=== FAQs ===
Q: What industries does Acme serve?
A: E-commerce, banking, healthcare, and telecom.
